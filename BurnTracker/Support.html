Getting Started

Welcome to BurnTracker, your all-in-one fitness and calorie tracking app. Here's how to get the most out of your experience:

Setting Up Your Profile

To get accurate calorie calculations, please complete your profile with your height, weight, age, and gender. This helps our app provide personalized metrics for your workouts.

Tracking Your First Workout

Starting a workout is simple:

  1. From the home screen, select your activity type
  2. Tap "Start" to begin tracking
  3. When finished, tap "End" to complete your workout
  4. Review your stats and save your workout

Apple Health Integration

BurnTracker can sync with Apple Health to provide a comprehensive view of your fitness data. To enable this feature, go to Profile > Settings > Health Integration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calorie calculations?
BurnTracker uses industry-standard MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) values combined with your personal profile data to calculate calories burned. For most activities, our calculations are within 10-15% accuracy compared to laboratory measurements.
Why does the app need my location?
Location access is only required for tracking distance, pace, and route during outdoor activities like running, walking, or cycling. You can still use the app without location permissions, but distance tracking will be less accurate.
Is my data stored in the cloud?
No. BurnTracker stores all your workout data locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal information on any servers.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual workouts by swiping left on any workout in your history. To completely remove all app data, you can uninstall the app from your device, as all data is stored locally.
Can I export my workout data?
While the app doesn't support direct export, if you enable Apple Health integration, your workout data will be available through the Health app.
How can I improve battery life while tracking?
For long workouts, you can reduce battery usage by:
  • Turning off the screen during workouts (tracking continues in the background)
  • Closing other apps running in the background
  • Starting with a fully charged device for long workouts

Troubleshooting

GPS Accuracy Issues

If you're experiencing inaccurate GPS tracking:

  • Ensure Location Services is set to "Always" or "While Using" for BurnTracker
  • Wait a few seconds after starting your workout for GPS to calibrate
  • Try to start workouts in open areas, away from tall buildings
  • Check if your device has the latest iOS update

App Crashing

If the app crashes or freezes:

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have the latest version of BurnTracker installed
  • If problems persist, contact our support team with details about your device and iOS version

HealthKit Sync Problems

If workouts aren't syncing with Apple Health:

  • Check that you've granted all necessary permissions in both BurnTracker and the Health app
  • Go to iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > BurnTracker and ensure all categories are enabled
  • Try toggling the Health integration off and on again in the app settings
