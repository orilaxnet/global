About SenticCut

What is SenticCut?

SenticCut is an AI-powered video editing app for iPhone and iPad, built by SynthesisLogic. Unlike traditional video editors that require manual timeline editing, SenticCut lets you describe your desired edit in plain text and the AI handles everything automatically.

The app uses advanced AI to analyze your video content, identify key moments, and generate a complete editing plan — including where to cut, which transitions to apply, color grading parameters, audio levels per segment, and dynamic speed changes. The entire process completes in under 30 seconds.

SenticCut is free to start with daily credits, requires no account, and never stores your videos on its servers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SenticCut?
SenticCut is an AI-powered video editing app for iOS (iPhone and iPad). You upload a video, type a text prompt describing your desired edit style, and the AI automatically applies smart cuts, transitions, color grading, audio adjustments, and speed changes. The entire process takes under 30 seconds. No manual timeline editing required.
Is SenticCut free to use?
Yes. SenticCut gives you free credits every day with no account required. You get full access to all AI editing features, transitions, effects, and export. For more edits, one-time credit packs start at $2.99 with no subscription and credits that never expire.
How does the AI edit my videos?
SenticCut uses Google Gemini AI to analyze your entire video. Based on your text prompt, the AI identifies key moments, decides where to cut, which transitions to use (dissolve, fade, cut), sets color grading (brightness, contrast, saturation), adjusts audio per segment (volume, fade-in, fade-out, muting), and applies dynamic speed changes. All automatically.
Is my video data private and secure?
Yes. SenticCut never stores your videos on its servers. Videos are transmitted via encrypted HTTPS for AI analysis and immediately discarded after processing. No email or login required. All API requests are signed with HMAC-SHA256 and verified with Apple DeviceCheck.
What devices are supported?
SenticCut requires iOS 17.0 or later and works on iPhone and iPad. Edited videos can be exported directly to your camera roll for sharing on any platform.
How is SenticCut different from CapCut, iMovie, or Premiere Rush?
Traditional editors require manual timeline editing with a steep learning curve. SenticCut is fully AI-driven. You describe what you want in plain text and AI handles everything — cuts, transitions, color, audio, speed. No timeline, no learning curve, and results in under 30 seconds.
