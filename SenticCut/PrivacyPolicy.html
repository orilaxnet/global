2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing your videos and prompts is necessary to provide the App's core service.
  • Legitimate Interest: Analytics and crash reporting help us maintain and improve the App.
  • Consent: Local notifications are only sent with your explicit permission.
  • Fraud Prevention: Device validation protects the integrity of the credit system.

2.2 Specific Uses

  • AI Video Processing: Videos and prompts are relayed to Google Gemini API for AI analysis. This is the core functionality of the App.
  • Credit Management: Your anonymous ID tracks your credit balance, purchase history, and daily free credit allocation.
  • Fraud Prevention: Apple DeviceCheck tokens help prevent abuse and automated exploitation of the credit system.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics helps us understand feature usage patterns to improve the App.
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics collects crash data to help us identify and fix bugs.
  • Local Notifications: If you grant permission, we send on-device notifications about daily credits and creative inspiration. These are local (on-device) notifications, not server-pushed.

2.3 What We Do NOT Do With Your Data

  • We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use your videos or prompts to train AI models.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising.
  • We do not share your data with data brokers.
  • We do not create advertising profiles based on your usage.

3. Data Processing & Retention

3.1 Video Content & Prompts

  • Videos and prompts are passed directly to Google Gemini API through our backend server for AI analysis.
  • We do not store, retain, copy, or archive your video content or prompts on our servers.
  • Our server acts solely as a secure encrypted relay (HTTPS) between your device and Google Gemini API.
  • After AI processing is complete, no video data or prompt data remains on our infrastructure.
  • We have no ability to access, review, or retrieve your video content after processing.

  • Firebase Analytics: Retained for 14 months per Google's data retention policy, then automatically deleted.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports retained for 90 days.
  • Google Gemini API: Subject to Google's data processing terms. We do not control Google's retention of data processed through their API.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Request Signing: Every API request is signed with HMAC-SHA256 to prevent tampering and unauthorized access.
  • Device Validation: Apple DeviceCheck tokens validate that requests originate from genuine Apple devices.
  • Keychain Storage: Your anonymous identifier is stored in the iOS Keychain, the most secure storage mechanism available on iOS.
  • No Plaintext Secrets: All sensitive credentials within the App are obfuscated.
Security Disclaimer: While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. TO THE FULLEST EXTENT PERMITTED BY APPLICABLE LAW, WE CANNOT AND DO NOT GUARANTEE ABSOLUTE SECURITY OF YOUR DATA. We are not responsible for unauthorized access to your data resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to device compromise, third-party breaches, or vulnerabilities in third-party services. You acknowledge that you provide your information at your own risk.

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Reset Your Identity

You can reset your anonymous user ID at any time through the App's Settings. This effectively creates a new user profile with no history. Note: This will irreversibly reset your credit balance. We bear no responsibility for credits or data lost as a result of a user-initiated reset.

6.2 Notification Control

You can enable or disable notifications at any time through iOS Settings. The App uses only local (on-device) notifications, not server-pushed notifications.

6.3 Photo Library Access

You can revoke photo library access at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy > Photos. The App requires this permission to function; revoking it will prevent you from selecting videos for editing.

6.4 Analytics Opt-Out

You can limit analytics collection through iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking. You may also disable analytics by restricting the App's network access.

6.5 Delete Your Data

Since we use anonymous identifiers and do not permanently store your video content:

  • Resetting your user ID in Settings effectively disassociates your identity from our servers.
  • For complete deletion of any server-side data associated with your UUID, contact us at the email below with your anonymous user ID (available in Settings).
  • We will process deletion requests within 45 days, as required by applicable California law.
