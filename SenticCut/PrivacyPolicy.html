1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

Data Purpose Storage
Anonymous User ID (UUID) Identify credit balance & usage Device Keychain (iCloud sync)
Device Check Token Fraud prevention Not stored (per-request)
App Usage Analytics Improve performance Firebase (Anonymized)
Crash Reports Bug fixing Firebase Crashlytics

1.2 Information You Provide

Data Purpose Storage
Video Content AI Analysis & Editing Passed to Google Gemini; Not stored
Text Prompts Guide AI decisions Passed to Google Gemini; Not stored

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We prioritize your privacy by not collecting:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Location data or Browsing history.
  • Contacts, Address book, or Health data.
  • Financial information (all payments handled by Apple).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Video Processing: Core functionality of the App.
  • Credit Management: Tracking balances and daily free allocations.
  • Fraud Prevention: Validating genuine device requests.
  • Crash Reporting: Ensuring stability and quick bug fixes.

3. Data Processing & Retention

3.1 Video Content & Prompts

Videos and prompts are passed directly to Google Gemini API through our backend server as a secure relay. We do not store or retain your video content on our servers. Data is encrypted via HTTPS and no video data remains on our infrastructure after processing.

3.2 What We DO Store

  • Anonymous User ID (UUID)
  • Purchase Records (Verification data from Apple StoreKit)
  • Credit Balance & User Tier Status

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • Google Gemini AI: Performance of AI-driven video analysis.
  • Apple Services: App Store, StoreKit, and DeviceCheck.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures including:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Request Signing: API requests signed with HMAC-SHA256.
  • Keychain Storage: Secure storage of identifiers on iOS.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You can Reset Your Identity at any time in the App Settings. This creates a new profile and resets your credit history. You also have full control over Photo Library access and Notifications via iOS System Settings.
