Frequently Asked Questions

I deleted the app and didn't add an email. Can I still access my data?
Yes! While it's more challenging without an email, we can still help. Contact us with your purchase information, device details, and any saved artwork you have. We'll do our best to identify your account and assist with your data rights.
How do you identify my account without email?
We use multiple methods: Apple purchase records (cross-referenced with your Apple ID), device information, usage patterns, and examples of your generated artwork. While email makes it easier, we have alternative identification methods for canvas and drawing apps.
What happens to my canvas projects after account deletion?
All canvas projects, drawings, generated artwork, and personal data are permanently removed within 30 days. Purchase records may be retained for legal compliance (tax purposes) for up to 7 years, but these are anonymized. Your creative work is permanently deleted unless you specifically request otherwise.
Can I get my data even if I'm not in the EU or California?
Yes! We provide data rights to all users regardless of location. We believe privacy is a fundamental right for everyone using our creative app.
How long do you take to process data requests?
We process all data rights requests within 30 days, as required by GDPR. Most requests are completed much faster. We'll send you confirmation and updates throughout the process.
Is there a fee for data requests?
No, all data rights requests are completely free. We never charge for providing access to your data or processing deletion requests.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí SketchGenius ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions through the App Store app.
How do credits work?
Credits are used to process your canvas artwork with AI. Each AI generation uses credits based on the complexity and quality settings. You can buy credits individually or get them with a subscription.
Are my canvas projects and drawings private?
Yes! Your canvas projects are completely private. Original canvas images used for AI processing are automatically deleted within 1 hour. We never share your creative work without your explicit permission.
What is AI generation and how does it work?
AI generation takes your canvas artwork (drawings, text, images) and applies artistic styles using artificial intelligence. Your canvas is temporarily processed to create new artistic versions. The original canvas data is deleted after processing, but the generated artwork is saved for you.
How do I get a refund?
We do not process refunds directly. All purchases are final and handled through Apple's App Store. For refund requests, contact Apple Support directly through the App Store or Apple's website.
Can I use my generated artwork commercially?
You own your generated artwork and can generally use it for personal or commercial purposes. However, you are fully responsible for ensuring your source content and final usage comply with all applicable laws and third-party rights.
Can I export my canvas projects?
Yes! You can export your canvas projects as images in various formats. You can also save them to your photo library or share them using iOS sharing features.
What drawing tools are available?
SketchGenius includes various drawing tools, pencils, brushes, and supports Apple Pencil with pressure sensitivity. You can also add text, import images, and create multi-layered projects.
Do you store my drawings on your servers?
Your drawings are primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync, projects may be backed up to our servers for cross-device access. You can control this in the app settings.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes! We follow strict security practices, use encryption for all data transmission, and store sensitive information using iOS Keychain security. We also comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.
What happens if I lose my device?
If you've enabled cloud sync, your projects will be available when you sign in on a new device. If not, projects stored only locally will be lost. We recommend enabling cloud backup for important projects.
Can I collaborate with others on projects?
Currently, SketchGenius is designed for individual creative work. You can share your finished projects with others, but real-time collaboration features are not available.
What file formats can I import/export?
You can import common image formats (JPEG, PNG, etc.) from your photo library. Export options include various image formats suitable for sharing, printing, or further editing in other apps.
Do you use my artwork to train AI models?
We do not use your personal artwork to train our own AI models. However, when using AI generation features, your canvas data is processed by OpenAI, which may use data to improve their services according to their terms of service.
