Frequently Asked Questions

SketchGenius is an iOS app that uses AI to transform your hand-drawn sketches into polished digital artwork. Draw on a canvas using Apple Pencil or your finger, choose an art style, add a text prompt, and the AI generates a finished piece based on your sketch in real-time.
Yes, SketchGenius is completely free to download. You receive free daily credits for AI generation. For heavier usage, you can subscribe to the Monthly Light plan ($3.99/month) or purchase one-time credit packs starting at $9.99.
Yes, SketchGenius fully supports Apple Pencil with pressure sensitivity and tilt detection. The drawing canvas is built on PencilKit for a native iOS drawing experience. You can also draw with your finger on any iPhone or iPad.
SketchGenius requires iOS 18.4 or later and works on iPhone and iPad. For the best drawing experience, we recommend using an iPad with Apple Pencil.
AI generation typically takes a few seconds. You can see a live streaming preview as the image is being created. The app uses real-time Server-Sent Events to show partial results during generation so you never stare at a blank loading screen.
Yes, all generated artwork is saved to your in-app library. You can also save images directly to your iOS photo library and share them through any app on your device.
