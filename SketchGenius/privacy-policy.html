Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SketchGenius ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). SketchGenius is a creative canvas and AI-powered drawing application that allows users to create digital artwork, add text and images, and transform their creations using artificial intelligence.

IMPORTANT: What SketchGenius Does and Does NOT Do

SketchGenius is a creative canvas and drawing application. We NEVER engage in face recognition, biometric data collection, or any form of facial analysis.

Our App Features:

  • Digital Canvas: Create artwork using drawing tools and Apple Pencil support
  • Multi-layer Projects: Combine drawings, text, and images in organized projects
  • AI Art Generation: Transform your canvas creations using artistic style templates
  • Project Management: Save, organize, and edit your creative projects
  • Style Application: Apply various artistic effects to your canvas artwork

What We NEVER Do:

  • Face Recognition: We do not identify, recognize, or analyze faces
  • Biometric Data Collection: We do not collect any biometric information
  • Personal Identification from Images: We do not analyze content for person identification
  • Face Tracking: We do not track or monitor facial features
  • Identity Analysis: We do not perform any form of identity verification through images

Technical Clarification:

SketchGenius processes your entire canvas artwork (drawings, text, and images) as artistic content for style transformation purposes only. This is similar to applying filters in photo editing apps - we process the visual content for artistic effects, not for identifying people or extracting biometric data.

Information We Collect

1. Canvas and Drawing Data

  • What we collect: Digital drawings created with Apple Pencil and touch input, canvas projects with multiple layers
  • How we use it: To save your projects, enable project management features, and provide canvas functionality
  • Storage: Projects stored locally on your device and optionally synchronized to our servers for backup
  • Processing: Canvas data is temporarily processed for AI generation when you request style transformations

2. AI Generation and Style Processing

  • What we collect: Canvas images you choose to process, selected style templates, and optional user prompts for enhancement
  • How we use it: To apply AI-powered artistic styles and generate transformed versions of your artwork
  • Storage: Original canvas images are temporarily stored for processing only and automatically deleted within 1 hour
  • Final results: Generated artwork is stored on our servers until you request deletion

3. Project Management Data

  • What we collect: Project names, creation dates, modification history, canvas size settings, and project metadata
  • How we use it: To organize your creative projects, provide project management features, and enable synchronization
  • Storage: Stored locally and optionally on our servers for cross-device access

4. Text and Image Elements

  • What we collect: Text content you add to canvas projects, images you incorporate into projects, and their positioning/styling data
  • How we use it: To render your projects correctly, save your creative work, and process them for AI generation when requested
  • Storage: Stored as part of your project data locally and optionally on our servers

5. Purchase and Subscription Data

  • What we collect: Purchase history, subscription status, credit balance, transaction information, and Apple Store receipts
  • How we use it: To manage your subscription, track credit usage, provide purchase-related features, and validate transactions
  • Storage: Stored securely on our servers and synchronized with Apple's StoreKit system
  • Third-party sharing: Shared with Apple for payment processing and subscription management only

6. Authentication and Account Data

  • What we collect: Encrypted authentication tokens, session data, and account identifiers
  • How we use it: To maintain secure app sessions, sync your data across devices, and provide personalized features
  • Storage: Stored using iOS Keychain security and automatically deleted when you log out

7. Device and Technical Information

  • What we collect: Device model, iOS version, app version, basic device identifiers, and performance metrics
  • How we use it: To ensure app compatibility, provide technical support, improve performance, and troubleshoot issues
  • Storage: Processed temporarily for technical purposes and aggregated anonymously for analytics

8. Usage and Analytics Information

  • What we collect: Feature usage patterns, canvas interactions, style selection preferences, and app performance data
  • How we use it: To improve app functionality, user experience, and service reliability
  • Storage: Processed locally and aggregated anonymously on our servers

Device Permissions

Photo Library Access

  • Reading permission: To import existing images into your canvas projects
  • Writing permission: To save your finished artwork and generated images to your photo library
  • User control: You can manage these permissions in iOS Settings at any time
  • Limited access: We only access specific photos you choose to import

Apple Pencil and Touch Input

  • Drawing input: To capture your drawing strokes and pressure sensitivity
  • Multi-touch gestures: For canvas navigation, zooming, and element manipulation
  • Local processing: All drawing input is processed locally on your device

Data Processing and AI Services

Local Canvas Processing

  • Most canvas operations (drawing, text editing, project management) are processed locally on your device
  • Your projects are stored locally unless you enable cloud synchronization
  • No canvas data leaves your device unless you specifically request AI generation or cloud backup

AI Image Generation Services

  • We use OpenAI's image generation services for AI-powered style transformation
  • Canvas images and style prompts are sent to OpenAI only when you request AI generation
  • Processing purpose: To apply artistic styles and effects to your canvas artwork
  • Data retention: OpenAI may retain data according to their usage policy (typically up to 30 days)
  • OpenAI usage: OpenAI may use data to improve their services subject to their terms
  • No personal identification: Only artistic content and style preferences are sent - no personal information
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard encryption
  • Automatic deletion: Original canvas images are deleted from our temporary storage within 1 hour

Style Templates and Processing

  • Template selection: Your chosen style templates and any custom prompts
  • Processing metadata: Canvas size, quality settings, and generation preferences
  • Result storage: Generated artwork is stored on our servers until you request deletion
  • No template monitoring: We do not monitor or review your style choices or generated content
