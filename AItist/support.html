Frequently Asked Questions

I deleted the app and didn't add an email. Can I still access my data?
Yes! While it's more challenging without an email, we can still help. Contact us with your purchase information, device details, and any generated images you saved. We'll do our best to identify your account and assist with your data rights.
How do you identify my account without email?
We use multiple methods: Apple purchase records (cross-referenced with your Apple ID), device information, usage patterns, and generated image examples you can provide. While email makes it easier, we have alternative identification methods.
What happens to my data after account deletion?
All personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Purchase records may be retained for legal compliance (tax purposes) for up to 7 years, but these are anonymized. Generated images are permanently deleted unless you specifically request otherwise.
Can I get my data even if I'm not in the EU or California?
Yes! We provide data rights to all users regardless of location. We believe privacy is a fundamental right for everyone using our app.
How long do you take to process data requests?
We process all data rights requests within 30 days, as required by GDPR. Most requests are completed much faster. We'll send you confirmation and updates throughout the process.
Is there a fee for data requests?
No, all data rights requests are completely free. We never charge for providing access to your data or processing deletion requests.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí AItist ‚Üí Cancel Subscription
How do credits work?
Credits are used to process images with AI. Each image transformation uses credits. You can buy credits or get them with a subscription.
Are my photos private?
Yes! Your original photos are automatically deleted within 1 hour after processing. We never share your images.
How do I get a refund?
We do not process refunds directly. All purchases are final and handled through Apple's App Store. For refund requests, contact Apple Support directly.
Can I use images commercially?
Yes! You own your images and can use them for personal or commercial purposes.
